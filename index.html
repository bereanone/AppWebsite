About

Biblical Heritage now maintains two connected study apps: Bible Readings 1914 (BRHC) and Biblical Heritage #StudyBible. Together they support structured Bible reading, curated study chains, commentary, and source library workflows.

BiblicalHeritage.net is the central home for app links, tutorials, shared lists, and future study downloads.

#StudyBible Features

  • Fast chapter and verse navigation with custom study workflows.
  • Custom word and range selection with copy, highlight, and Strong's shortcuts.
  • Structured sharing using #tags and $tags.
  • $tags support ordered study chains with notes between linked scriptures.
  • Commentary tools support imported PDF source works and metadata-driven study excerpts.
  • Bible Memory tools support curated verses, ranges, and grouped study review.
