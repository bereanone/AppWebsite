About

Biblical Heritage is building a carefully curated KJV Study Bible designed for clarity, accuracy, and reverence for the inspired text. Our mission is to preserve Scripture-centered resources in digital form while remaining faithful to the historic Word of God.

The KJV Study Bible is currently in active development. Additional historic volumes, reference works, and study resources are planned.
